The Roadmap and Agenda Development Process

An interactive, consultative process is in progress to develop the national logistics research agenda and roadmap. This online data collection tool provides an open platform by which stakeholders can contribute their perspectives on what we do not yet know, and what we should know more of, to inform our understanding of the national logistics system and to facilitate improvement. The steps are as follows:

  1. Online Data Collection
  2. Small Focus Group Sessions
  3. Version 1 Roadmap
  4. Expert Review
  5. Final Roadmap and Agenda


The online data collection process is augmented by small focus group discussions to enhance the collected and collective understanding, and to shape a guiding agenda and roadmap. Expert review of the Version 1 Roadmap will lead to the publication of the final agenda and roadmap.
